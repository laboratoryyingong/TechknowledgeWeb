Why Choose Techknowledge Institute?

Established in South Australia in 2003. We provide a number of IT related services. We have developed web and mobile applications for startUps, companies and government.
Our latest project is an innovation of our own. TKiEE (www.TKiEE.com) has recently received funding to move to the commercialisation phase and was selected in the IBM Global Entrepreneur Program and microsoft bizspark program.

How can we Help

We provide a number of services, including research services, consulting, software applications development.
We specialize in helping organisations ‘test’ their ideas through the development of innovation prototypes. We can help you design and develop a prototype application which can bring your idea to life without having to invest large amounts of money.
